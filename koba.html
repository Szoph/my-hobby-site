Koba

Meet Koba!

Koba is a mini rex rabbit and is 5 years old. He is really shy and likes to feel safe in tunnels and houses. He is an indoor rabbit that shares his room with his brother Caesar, but would much prefer to be alone. He used to like to pull the wallpaper off in his room before we had to take it off!

His favourite foods include bananas, dill, kale, mint and coriander. He loves to snuggle and likes soft beds to sleep on but is really territorial when it comes to sharing.

Koba is extremely agile and can jump over safety gates so a watchful eye is necessary with this escape artist. He didn't like having his nails cut but requires very little help with grooming as he takes care of this himself.
