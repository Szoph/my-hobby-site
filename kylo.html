Kylo

Meet Kylo!

Kylo is a 3 year old Boxer dog. He was named after the Star Wars character 'Kylo Ren'. His nicknames are:

  • Ky
  • Stinky Bum
  • Pooper
  • Ky Dog

When he was a puppy, he would chew anything he could. He would also bury things in the garden such as TV remotes!

His favourite things are chicken, car rides, walkies, fetch, sunny weather and barking at strangers.

His least favourite things are the postman, unknown items in the house, lemons, being told "no" and birds in the garden.
