Caesar

Meet Caesar!

Caesar is a lionhead rabbit. He is 6 years old and is very fluffy. He's an indoor rabbit and has his own room which he shares with his brother Koba. He will chew any wires that are unattended (luckily he's always unharmed!)

His favourite foods include blueberries, dill, coriander, carrots and mint. He loves to snack on these but also likes to chew on cardboard, skirting boards and toilet paper.

Because he is a lionhead his fur can get unmanageable if he's not groomed regularly. He loves to be brushed and will lay patiently, but doesn't tolerate having his nails cut and will protest.
